History

Scroll

Bayyinah Begins

Using a laptop and an Internet connection, Pakistani-American Nouman Ali Khan launches Bayyinah. He also begins teaching Fundamentals of Classical Arabic in New York and New Jersey. Soon after, he starts teaching Tajwid and Reading Essentials with Wisam Sharieff.

Nationwide seminars

A spurt of nation-wide seminars, lectures and public speeches takes Bayyinah to more than 40 locations across the USA.

A partnership

Sheikh Abdul Nasir Jangda joins Nouman Ali Khan’s Bayyinah and teaches his first course [IN WHAT] in Vermont.

meaningful prayer

Research completed for Bayyinah’s Meaningful Prayer course, one of the institute’s longest-running courses.

launch of quran for families

Bayyinah launches the “Quran for Families” series with Surah Ar-Rahman presented by Nouman Ali Khan and Surah Ya-Sin presented by Sheikh Abdul Nasir Jangda.

bayyinah dream launches

Classes begin for male and female students. The Bayyinah Dream Program, a 9-month intensive Arabic immersion course, is launched with the institute’s inaugural class of 65 students at the new campus.

Quran intensive

Bayyinah launches a 30-day intensive Quran studies program taught by Sheikh Abdul Nasir Jangda in Carrollton, Texas. It is the subject of a documentary series.

website launch

Bayyinah TV’s website launch is announced at the Amazed by the Quran conference with recordings of Arabic with Husna, Quran: Cover to Cover and Divine Speech.

singapore convention

Amazed by the Quran convention held in Singapore in November.

ramadan daily program

A daily tafseer of Surah Al-Baqarah begins at the Euless Masjid in Ramadan, June 2016.

first live webinar

Nouman Ali Khan presents Bayyinah TV’s first live webinar to a live audience across the globe in May.
