Bayyinah Institute

 __

Bayyinah Institute is the brainchild of Ustadh Nouman Ali Khan, a pioneering, influential Pakistani-American Muslim who grew up in New York.

Based in Dallas, Texas, Bayyinah’s mission is to make learning the Arabic language and the study of the Quran accessible to the world in a way that is innovative, affordable and easy to understand.

Bayyinah’s founder believes that using modern technology and cutting edge, educational tools is key to spreading an appreciation of Arabic and Islam’s sacred texts to those who might not have the opportunity to commit to rigorous study overseas.

Bayyinah TV

__

Bayyinah TV is a subscription-based video collection of over 2,000 exclusive Quran tafseer and Arabic teaching programs. Each series is a comprehensive educational experience. Among others, the offerings include: “Arabic with Husna” for teaching Arabic grammar; “A Concise Commentary,” a complete interpretation by Nouman Ali Khan; “Divine Speech,” an eye-opening deep dive into the literary genius of Allah’s words; and “A Thematic Overview,” a series which delves into the recurrent themes in the Quran.

Bayyinah Dream

__

The Dream program is Bayyinah’s flagship nine-month, full-time Arabic and Quran studies program. Conducted on site at the Bayyinah Campus, the program helps students learn and apply grammar, internalize extensive vocabulary, and enhance their Quran comprehension skills.

Access Program

__

Catering to the needs of students with busy schedules and a modern-day lifestyle, the Access Program is a live three-tier online Arabic studies course for part-time learners with demanding commitments. It is available to students all over the world.

Quran Intensive

__

Offered annually on site in Dallas, Texas, Bayyinah’s Quran Intensive is a popular 30-day Arabic and Quran studies program for the whole family. It combines an extensive Quranic Arabic curriculum with Quran analysis lectures. Great use of summer vacation!

Traveling Seminars

__

Children, adults and non-Muslims are welcome to sign up for these engaging one or two-day seminars presented by Bayyinah’s Instructors across the United States and internationally. Their light, easy-going atmosphere are a great way to spend the weekend and mingle with like-minded individuals

Podcast

__

Bayyinah’s free audio library available in Apple and Andriod apps offers a thorough literary study of the entire Quran, khutbahs by Nouman Ali Khan and other sheikhs as well as selected lectures, all free of charge. The library is updated regularly and is one of Bayyinah’s most popular services, reaching two terabytes of downloads daily.
